About Gotham’s Defender

Batman stands as one of the most iconic comic heroes. He doesn’t rely on superpowers; instead, he uses intelligence, discipline, and technology to protect Gotham City. His story proves that strength and greatness can come from determination and purpose.

Many admire Batman because he pushes through fear and challenges. His courage, training, and willpower inspire fans to believe in justice and never quit, no matter the odds.
