About Aliyah

I’m Aliyah Phothilat, a licensed esthetician based in Chattanooga, Tennessee. I started Lia Luxe Co. for people who want healthy skin and soft glam without feeling overwhelmed or judged.

My approach is gentle, honest, and realistic. I don’t believe in ten-step routines you’ll never keep up with. I like to keep things simple, calm, and focused on what actually works for your skin.

Quick Skin Care Tips

  • 1. Be gentle with cleansing. Avoid harsh scrubs every day. A soft cleanser and lukewarm water are enough for most skin.
  • 2. Moisturize, even if you’re oily. Skipping moisturizer can make your skin feel tight and reactive. Choose a lightweight option if you’re oily or acne-prone.
  • 3. Don’t pick at breakouts. Picking can lead to dark marks and more irritation. Let extractions happen in the treatment room instead.
  • 4. Sunscreen is non-negotiable. Daily SPF helps protect your results and prevents dark spots and early fine lines.
  • 5. Consistency beats perfection. A simple routine you actually do will always beat a complicated one you quit after a week.
