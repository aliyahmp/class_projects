Skin Care Tips

Simple, realistic advice to help your skin look and feel better between appointments.

Aliyah’s Approach

I’m Aliyah Phothilat, a licensed esthetician in Chattanooga, Tennessee. At Lia Luxe Co., I focus on gentle treatments, clean ingredients, and routines that are actually doable in real life.

These tips are a starting point. They don’t replace a full consultation, but they can keep your skin calmer, softer, and healthier between visits.

Quick Everyday Tips

  • 1. Keep cleansing gentle. Avoid harsh scrubs every day. A soft cleanser and lukewarm water are enough for most skin.
  • 2. Moisturize daily. Even oily and acne-prone skin needs hydration. Choose a lightweight, non-comedogenic moisturizer.
  • 3. Don’t pick. Picking at breakouts can cause dark marks and texture. Let extractions happen in the treatment room instead.
  • 4. Wear sunscreen. Daily SPF helps protect your results, prevent dark spots, and slow early fine lines.
  • 5. Keep it simple. A basic routine you actually do (cleanser, moisturizer, SPF) is better than a 10-step routine you give up on.

Before Your Appointment

  • Avoid strong at-home peels or scrubs a few days before your facial.
  • Come with a clean face if possible (no heavy makeup).
  • Write down any products you’re currently using so we can look at them together.

After Your Appointment

  • Skip harsh exfoliants or retinoids for a couple of days unless we talk about it first.
  • Stay hydrated and use a gentle moisturizer to support your skin barrier.
  • Try not to touch your face too much right after extractions or treatments.
