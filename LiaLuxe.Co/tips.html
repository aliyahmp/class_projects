Skin Care Tips

Simple, realistic tips from Aliyah to keep your skin calm, glowy, and happy.

About Aliyah

I’m Aliyah Phothilat, a licensed esthetician and owner of Lia Luxe Co. in Chattanooga, Tennessee. My style is simple: cozy vibes, calm energy, and skin-first beauty that fits real life.

Whether you’re dealing with acne, dryness, or you just want a soft glam glow, my goal is to make your routine feel less stressful and more doable.

Before Your Facial

  • Arrive with a bare face if you can. If not, I’ll remove your makeup for you.
  • Avoid strong exfoliants or retinoids 2–3 days before your facial.
  • Drink water and eat something light so you feel comfortable during your appointment.

After Your Facial

  • Skip heavy makeup and intense workouts for the rest of the day.
  • Be gentle with your skin — no scrubs or harsh products for a few days.
  • Use hydration and SPF to protect your results.

Everyday Skin Tips

  • 1. Keep your routine simple. A cleanser, moisturizer, and SPF can already do a lot.
  • 2. Don’t chase every trend. Not every viral product is right for your skin type.
  • 3. Be consistent. Doing a small routine daily beats doing a big one once a week.
  • 4. Protect your barrier. If your skin feels tight, burning, or stingy, it probably needs soothing and moisture.
  • 5. Ask questions. During your appointment, I’m happy to help you pick products that actually make sense for you.
