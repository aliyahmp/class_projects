The Dark Knight of Gotham

Defender of Justice

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is the protector of Gotham City. He uses his intellect, detective skills, and advanced technology to combat crime and corruption. His story is one of tragedy and purpose, symbolizing the fight between justice and chaos.

From his iconic Batmobile to his unbreakable moral code, Batman stands as one of the most inspiring figures in superhero history. His presence in Gotham reminds every villain that crime will never go unpunished.
